Shaping Great Minds

Today's Gen Z learners grow in a fast-changing world where creativity, emotional intelligence, cultural awareness, and innovation are as important as academic excellence.

Early exposure to diverse forms of arts, sciences, and creative exploration helps children discover their unique strengths, interests, and aptitudes.

The Vacation Arts & Exploration Program is designed as a 45-day immersive experience. The goal is not merely to introduce students to many activities, but to help them discover areas where they can truly excel and eventually specialize.

Who Can Join?

Students from class I - XII

  • Wish to explore creative talents
  • Experience performing and visual arts
  • Build confidence and self-expression
  • Discover their natural aptitude and interests
